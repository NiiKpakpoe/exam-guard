Before you start — checklist

  • Use a laptop or desktop with Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (not a phone).
  • Connect to stable internet — wired or strong Wi-Fi.
  • Charge your device or keep it plugged in for the full exam length.
  • Find a quiet, private space where you won't be interrupted.
  • Close other apps, tabs, and notifications (chat, email, music).
  • If a webcam check-in is required, make sure your camera works and you allow access.
  • Have your name and student/candidate ID ready.

How to take the exam

  1. Click Join exam above and enter your access code.
  2. Enter your full name and student ID.
  3. Accept the honour code (and take the check-in photo if asked).
  4. Click Begin exam in fullscreen. The screen goes fullscreen — stay there.
  5. Answer the questions. Watch the timer at the top right.
  6. Click Submit when done (or it submits automatically when time runs out).

Rules while the exam is running

Your activity is monitored and logged. The following are recorded and shown to your instructor — repeated violations can auto-submit your exam:

  • Stay in fullscreen. Leaving fullscreen is flagged.
  • Don't switch tabs or windows. Clicking away from the exam is flagged.
  • No copy, paste, or right-click. These are disabled and logged.
  • One attempt only. You cannot retake the exam once submitted.
  • Questions and answer order may be shuffled — yours won't match a classmate's.

Troubleshooting

The camera won't turn on
Click the camera icon in the browser address bar and choose Allow, then retake the photo. Use Chrome or Edge.
It says "wrong access code"
Check for spaces or typos and confirm the exact code with your instructor.
I accidentally left fullscreen
Go back into fullscreen immediately (the warning banner tells you). It's logged, so don't do it repeatedly.
My internet dropped
Reconnect and rejoin with the same code quickly. Note: you get one attempt, so contact your instructor if you were locked out before submitting.
The page froze
Don't refresh repeatedly. Wait a moment; if needed, reload once and continue. Tell your instructor if your answers were lost.
"Already submitted"
This exam allows a single attempt per student ID. Contact your instructor if this is a mistake.
